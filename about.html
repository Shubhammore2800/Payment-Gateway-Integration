The Mission of Feed God’s
Hungry Children

Our Mission Feed God’s Hungry Children reaches out to hurting children and their families all over the world by providing food, clothing, and spiritual education. You can be a part of helping and healing these forgotten and hurting children. You can make a difference in the world and give hope to these hopeless children. Please consider supporting the good work of Feed God’s Hungry children today, and you can know that you will be part of a much better tomorrow.

We believe in collaboration as we know this work is bigger than any one organization. We are part of a global network of nonprofit organizations leading the worldwide movement.
